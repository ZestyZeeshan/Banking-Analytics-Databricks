Key Concepts Learned
Dashboard

Collection of charts and KPIs used for business reporting.

KPI (Key Performance Indicator)

A metric used to measure business performance.

Examples:

Total Deposits
Total Loans
Fraud Alerts
Total Transactions
Visualization

Graphical representation of data.

Examples:

Bar Chart
Pie Chart
Table
Business Storytelling

Converting data into meaningful business insights.

Example:

Raw Data:
Branch A = ₹1,20,000

Business Insight:
Branch A is the highest-performing branch.
Why Gold Layer for Dashboards?

Because Gold Layer contains:

Aggregated Data
Business Metrics
Reporting KPIs

and is optimized for reporting.


What is a Dashboard?

A dashboard is a collection of charts and KPIs used to monitor business performance.

What is a KPI?

A measurable business metric used for performance tracking.

Why use Gold tables for dashboards?

Because Gold tables contain business-ready aggregated data.

What dashboards did you create?

Customer Analysis, Branch Analysis, Loan Analysis, Transaction Analysis, and Fraud Risk Monitoring.

What is Business Storytelling?

Transforming raw data into actionable business insights.

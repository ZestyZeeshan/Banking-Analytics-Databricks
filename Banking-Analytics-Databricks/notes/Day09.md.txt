What is Data Lineage?

Tracks data movement from source to final dashboard.

What is Medallion Architecture?

A layered architecture consisting of Bronze, Silver, and Gold layers for data processing.

What is Delta Lake?

A storage layer that provides reliability, ACID transactions, and performance optimizations.

Why use Bronze, Silver, and Gold layers?

To separate raw, cleansed, and business-ready data for better maintainability and scalability.

Relationships:

Customer -> Account -> Transaction

-> Why separate Customer and Account tables?

Answer: A customer can have multiple accounts, so storing them separately reduces redundancy and improves data management.

Customer -> Loan

Why separate Transactions?

-> Answer: Transactions grow rapidly and represent a different business entity, making separate storage more scalable and efficient.
